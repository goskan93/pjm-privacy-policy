Privacy Policy — PJM

Last updated: June 2026

PJM is a Polish Sign Language dictionary app. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

Third parties

We do not share any data with third parties beyond Firebase/Google as described above.

Children

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
